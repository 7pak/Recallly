Recallly - Account & Data Deletion

Recallly is an offline-first app. All your data (voice notes, extracted fields, custom fields, and preferences) is stored locally on your device.

How to Delete Your Account & Data

  1. Open the Recallly app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

This will permanently delete:

  • Your Firebase authentication account
  • All voice notes and transcriptions
  • All extracted field data
  • Custom fields
  • App preferences and settings
  • Google Drive backups (if enabled)

This action cannot be undone.
